Best movies according to me

My top movies of all time

The legend

The Legend is a 2015 crime thriller starring Tom Hardy in dual roles as the infamous Kray twins, Ronnie and Reggie.

Set in 1960s London, the film follows their rise to power as gangsters who dominated the East End.

Hardy's brilliant performance brings both brothers to life with distinct personalities—Reggie, the suave and calculated leader, and Ronnie, the volatile and unpredictable one.

The movie delves into their complex relationship, exploring loyalty, betrayal, and the violent world they ruled.

Hardy's portrayal is the heart of the film, earning critical praise for its depth and intensity.

Mr. Robot

Mr. Robot is a critically acclaimed psychological thriller TV series that delves into the mind of Elliot Alderson, a brilliant but troubled cybersecurity engineer played by Rami Malek.
Elliot is recruited by the mysterious anarchist Mr. Robot, played by Christian Slater, to join a hacker group aiming to dismantle the world’s largest corporation.
The series explores themes of identity, control, and revolution, while unraveling complex narratives about mental health and societal corruption.
With its gripping storytelling, intricate plot twists, and unique visual style, *Mr. Robot* became a cultural phenomenon, blending cyberpunk elements with emotional depth.

John wick

The John Wick series follows Keanu Reeves as John Wick, a retired hitman forced back into the violent world of assassins after a personal tragedy.

Across four films, John seeks vengeance for the killing of his dog and becomes the target of a global contract, fighting against deadly foes and a powerful crime syndicate known as The High Table.

With each installment, John faces relentless enemies, old alliances, and a brutal struggle for freedom, all while trying to escape the dark legacy of his violent past.

The series is renowned for its intense action and world-building.
